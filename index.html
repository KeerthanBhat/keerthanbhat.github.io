Keerthan Bhat

software developer • machine learning engineer • toastmaster • grasshopper

Hi there!

I'm a Class of 2020 graduate in Computer Science & Engineering from R.V. College of Engineering. I enjoy working with data to build robust systems and develop products which enhance the lives of customers. Currently, I'm interning as a Data Scientist at Curl Analytics. I'm having a great time working on text data from commodity trading companies for various tasks such as classification, language inference and sequence labelling.

During my final semester at college, I interned at Samsung Research Institute, Bangalore (SRIB) for my undergraduate thesis where I worked on deep learning models for the enhancement of Time-of-Flight (ToF) depth images. I visited SRIB during the summer before this too, to work on building a video analysis pipeline for multi-modal emotion classification. A year before that, I got my first work experience & startup exposure at ZoomTail where I was the first hire in the engineering team and I built the first version of their tech. platform end to end.

During my undergraduate days, along with my team, I won & lost many hackathons, b-plan contests, was a speaker curator for TEDx RVCE and worked in a nano-satellite building team on multiple functions. I take keen interest in product management, user-research and follow startups' news regularly. I also exercise my public speaking and leadership skills through Toastmasters. I tend to read non-fiction books and write regularly (okay, not so regularly!). My hobbies keep changing from time to time but right now, I exploring the realms of Chess & Birding!
